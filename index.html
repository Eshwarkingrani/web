Who I Am

Hi, I am Eshwar Washdev Kingrani, a passionate student and aspiring tech entrepreneur from Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan. I am currently studying in 12th class under the Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education Larkana. I achieved an A star in my matric and scored 91 percent in my 11th class.

My Background

I am also a former cadet. I studied my 7th, 8th and 9th classes at Cadet College Karampur. During this time, I served as the second class representative in 7th class, supporting the main CR, and later became the class representative in both 8th and 9th classes. My time at cadet college helped me develop strong skills in public speaking, discipline, leadership and management. I was also honored with the Speaker of the Year award in English.
